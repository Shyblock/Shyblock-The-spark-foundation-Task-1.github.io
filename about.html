History of Shreya's Bank

->Shreya's Bank was originally promoted in 2000 , an Indian financial institution, and was its wholly-owned subsidiary.

->Shreya's Bank offers a wide range of banking products and financial services to corporate and retail customers through a variety of delivery channels and through its group companies.
